Hello My Name is Kristen De La Rosa

Designer & Front-end Developer.

My name is Kristen De La Rosa and I have spent 8 years in the classroom educating our youth! Teachers are the best kinds of people. They have so many qualities that outshine most professions. So what qualities do I have that will make me the best person you will ever know? Felixbility, muti-tasking, due deligence, and time management come second nature to me, these are the skills that not only allow me to manage a classroom of 25 teenagers but are imperative to survival.

Imagine yourself lost in the woods unaware of your surroundings, or the chances of rescue. Naturally in this incident you would want someone like Bear Grylls by your side, or the very least a boy scout. The third best option would be me. I have already planned ahead for this very situation! The first thing I will address would be our immediate goals, example, get out of the woods. I would address each of our strengths and deligate our respoinsibities setting clear expectations and articulating very procedural directions, making sure to ask if there are any questions. How will this save us? Well, you will be so distracted with your job at hand I will use that time to use my fully charged cell phone to contact emergency services, because remember I already planned for this.
